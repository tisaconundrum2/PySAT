libpysat.derived.crism.crism.bdi1000IR(data, w_array)[source]¶
NAME: BDI1000IR

PARAMETER: 1 micron integrated band depth; IR wavelengths FORMULATION *: divide R1030, R1050, R1080, R1150

by linear fit from peak R between 1.3 - 1.87 microns to R2530 extrapolated backwards, then integrate over (1 - normalized radiances)
RATIONALE: crystalline Fe+2 minerals; corrected for overlying
